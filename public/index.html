About Us

We are committed to equipping and empowering educators, school leaders, caregivers and education practitioners in Montessori Education. We offer our students qualitative training that involves comprehensive knowledge of the Montessori pedagogy, which conforms to the international Montessori teacher education standard that you can obtain anywhere in the world.

Driven by a common passion to help children harness their skills and maximise their potential, we are dedicated to providing qualitative training to all those who wish to work in early childhood settings, applying the Montessori method of education. Our top priority is to ensure that the knowledge, training, information and materials relating to the Montessori approach to education are accessible to everyone. Our centre is conducive to learning with developmentally appropriate materials to give students hands-on experience in Montessori philosophy and practice.

Our Mission

To offer and provide qualitative Montessori training to educators and other practitioners to equip and empower educational practitioners and caregivers with the knowledge, skills and resources necessary to meet educational and development requirements of children globally.

Our Vision

To be a premium and preferred service provider of Montessori Teachers’ Training, and to rank among the best in the industry.
